About Me

My name is Andy David Zurita García. I'm twenty years old and I come from Mexico. I've been living in Montreal for almost six years now. It's been quite a journey adapting to a new country and culture, but I've found it incredibly rewarding.

About two months ago, I decided to dive into the world of programming, and I've been making significant progress ever since. It's been a passion of mine for years, and I'm thrilled to finally be pursuing it more formally.

Studying programming has been both challenging and exciting. I've been taking online courses and dedicating a lot of time to self-directed learning. With the support of my family and my own determination, I've been able to improve my skills and expand my knowledge in a relatively short amount of time.

Beyond programming, I love exploring the vibrant city of Montreal. There's always something new to discover, whether it's a hidden café or a beautiful park. I also enjoy spending time outdoors, hiking in the nearby mountains or biking along the river.

I'm incredibly excited about the opportunities that lie ahead in the field of programming. My dream is to work on innovative projects that have a positive impact on the world. With hard work and perseverance, I know I can achieve my goals and make a difference in the tech industry.
